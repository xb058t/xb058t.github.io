Warranty Information

Thank you for choosing Ramsey Ind! We are committed to delivering high-quality keyboards, and your satisfaction is our priority. Please read the following information regarding our warranty policy.

Warranty Coverage

All keyboards purchased from Ramsey Ind come with a standard warranty. The warranty period varies by product, and specific details can be found in the product description.

What the Warranty Covers

Our warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. If your keyboard exhibits such issues during the warranty period, we will repair or replace the product at our discretion.

Exclusions from Warranty

The warranty does not cover damage caused by accidents, misuse, modifications, unauthorized repairs, or any other circumstances not arising from defects in materials or workmanship.
